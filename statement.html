The Consequences of Food Waste:

Environmental Impact: When food is wasted, this needlessly squanders resources. A surprising amount of water is used for food production. For example, wasting a pound of cheese is the equivalent of running the shower for 122 minutes. Food waste in landfills generates methane, which is more damaging to the environment than carbon dioxide.

Financial and Social Impact: Americans discard about $165 billion every year worth of food. The could be up to $2,200 per household. The entire population of hungry people in the world, 870 million, could be fed by the food wasted by consumers and retailers.
